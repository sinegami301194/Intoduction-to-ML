How to install Nvidia Graphics Driver on Ubuntu 18.04 instead of nouveau:

1) System settings → Details → Owerview
	Check “Graphics”
2) Open “Software & Updates”
3) Click “Additional Drivers”
4) Click “Using NVIDIA driver metapackage from nvidia-driver-390 (property, tested)”
5) Click “Apply Changes”
6) Reboot your computer
7) System settings → Details → Owerview
	“Graphics” has changed
